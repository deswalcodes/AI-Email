# Northwind Gear — Customer Support Knowledge Base

> Mock company KB for the AI Email Automation prototype. Northwind Gear is a fictional
> e-commerce retailer of outdoor & fitness equipment. Each `## ` section becomes one
> retrievable KB article.

## Shipping & Delivery Timelines [Delivery Issue]
Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the country and 7–14 business days
internationally. Express shipping (1–2 business days) is available at checkout for an
extra fee. Orders placed before 2 PM local time ship the same business day. A tracking
number is emailed automatically once the parcel leaves our warehouse. If tracking has
not updated for more than 5 business days, the parcel is considered delayed and the
customer is eligible for a re-ship or full refund.

## Lost or Missing Parcels [Delivery Issue]
If a parcel is marked "delivered" but the customer has not received it, ask them to
check with neighbours and their building mailroom first, then wait 24 hours as carriers
sometimes scan early. If still missing, we open a carrier investigation (resolved within
3–5 business days) and offer the customer the choice of a free replacement or a full
refund. Customers never pay for re-shipment of a lost parcel.

## Return & Exchange Policy [Return / Refund]
Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided they are
unused and in original packaging. Worn or used items are eligible for store credit only.
To start a return the customer needs an RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) number,
which we generate from the dashboard. Return shipping is free for defective items; for
change-of-mind returns a flat $6 return label fee is deducted from the refund.

## RMA Numbers & Return Tracking [Return / Refund]
An RMA number has the format RMA-XXXXXX. It is required on the return shipping label.
Refunds are processed within 5 business days of the warehouse receiving the returned
item and appear on the customer's statement 3–7 business days later depending on their
bank. Customers can check return status by replying with their RMA number.

## Refund Processing Times [Return / Refund]
Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Card refunds take 5–10 business days
to appear; PayPal refunds are usually instant to 24 hours. If more than 10 business days
have passed and no refund is visible, escalate to the billing team for a payment trace.

## Billing, Invoices & Overcharges [Billing]
Invoices are emailed at the time of purchase and are downloadable from the customer's
account under Orders → Invoice. If a customer reports being charged twice, the second
charge is almost always a temporary authorization hold that drops off within 3–5
business days. Genuine duplicate charges are refunded immediately once confirmed. For
subscription billing, customers can pause or cancel anytime before the renewal date to
avoid the next charge.

## Subscription Management [Billing]
The Northwind+ membership ($9/month) gives free express shipping and early access to
sales. It renews monthly and can be cancelled anytime from Account → Membership with no
cancellation fee. Cancelling stops future charges but does not refund the current month.

## Account Access & Login Problems [Product Issue]
If a customer cannot log in, first have them reset their password via the "Forgot
password" link. If reset emails are not arriving, check the spam folder and confirm the
email on file. Accounts lock after 5 failed attempts and auto-unlock after 30 minutes.
For "account not found" errors the customer likely registered with a different email.

## App Crashes & Technical Bugs [Product Issue]
For app crashes, advise customers to update to the latest version, restart the device,
and clear the app cache. Known issue: the v4.2 Android build crashes on the checkout
screen — the fix is to update to v4.3+. Always collect the device model, OS version, and
app version so engineering can reproduce. Persistent bugs are logged as tickets and the
customer is given a tracking reference.

## Product Information & Availability [General Enquiry]
Product specifications, sizing charts, and material details are on each product page.
Out-of-stock items show an estimated restock date and a "notify me" option. Our support
hours are Monday–Friday 9 AM–6 PM and Saturday 10 AM–4 PM. Live chat is available during
support hours; email is monitored 24/7 by the automation system.

## Warranty Coverage [Product Issue]
All Northwind Gear products carry a 1-year limited warranty against manufacturing
defects. Warranty claims require proof of purchase and photos of the defect. Approved
claims are resolved with a free repair, replacement, or refund at our discretion.
Normal wear and tear, misuse, and accidental damage are not covered.

## Legal, Privacy & Data Requests [Legal]
GDPR / data-access and data-deletion requests must be acknowledged within 72 hours and
fulfilled within 30 days — these are always routed to a human on the legal team and never
auto-answered. Mentions of lawsuits, solicitors/attorneys, regulatory bodies (FTC, ICO),
chargebacks framed as disputes, or threats of legal action are treated as legally
sensitive and escalated immediately. Support agents must not admit liability in writing.

## Price Match & Discounts [General Enquiry]
We price-match identical in-stock items from major authorized retailers within 7 days of
purchase — the customer sends a link to the lower price and we refund the difference.
First-time customers get 10% off with code WELCOME10. Discount codes cannot be stacked.

## Damaged on Arrival [Product Issue]
If an item arrives damaged, ask for photos of the item and packaging. Confirmed
damaged-on-arrival items get an immediate free replacement or full refund with no return
required, and we cover all shipping costs. This is treated as a priority case.
